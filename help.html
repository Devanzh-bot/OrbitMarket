General Questions

👤 Account & Login

How do I create an account?

Visit our login page and click on "Sign Up". Fill in your details including email, password, and phone number. Verify your email, and you're ready to shop!

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your registered email, and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email.

How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account and go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings". You can update your name, email, phone number, and delivery address anytime.

📦 Products & Shopping

How can I search for products?

Use the search bar on the Shop page. You can search by product name, category, or code. Filter by category and price range to narrow down results.

Are the prices updated in real-time?

Yes! Product prices are updated regularly. Prices may vary based on current stock and promotions. Final price is confirmed in your cart before checkout.

How do I know if a product is in stock?

Each product listing shows stock availability. A green checkmark means the item is in stock. Out of stock items are marked accordingly.

Can I compare products?

Yes! Click the "Compare" button on product cards to view detailed comparison information including price, category, and specifications.

📋 Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 2-3 business days. Express delivery is available for 1-day delivery on orders placed before 2 PM. Delivery charges may apply.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is confirmed, you'll receive a tracking number via email. Use it on our "Track Order" page to see real-time delivery updates.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to 100+ locations across the city and surrounding areas. Enter your pincode at checkout to check delivery availability in your area.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, please contact our support team. Once dispatched, orders cannot be cancelled.

💳 Payments & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), net banking, UPI, and cash on delivery for selected locations.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes! We use 256-bit SSL encryption and PCI-DSS compliance. Your payment details are never stored on our servers. All transactions are secure.

How can I apply a promo code?

Enter your promo code in the "Apply Coupon" field during checkout. The discount will be applied instantly. Check our Offers page for active promo codes.

Do you offer EMI options?

Yes! EMI is available on credit cards for purchases above $500. Select your preferred bank and tenure at checkout.

↩️ Returns & Refunds

What's your return policy?

Most items can be returned within 7 days of delivery if unopened/unused. Perishable items have a 24-hour return window. Contact support for return authorization.

How long do refunds take?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive and verify the returned item. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

What if I receive a damaged product?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damaged item. We'll either replace it or refund you. No return shipping is required for damaged goods.

Can I exchange a product?

Yes! Exchange is available for defective items within 48 hours. For size/variant changes, you can return and place a new order for a different product.

❓ General Questions

Do you offer a loyalty program?

Yes! Join our loyalty program to earn points on every purchase. Points can be redeemed for discounts. Members get exclusive deals, birthday offers, and free delivery.

Are there any hidden charges?

No hidden charges! All costs including taxes and delivery are shown clearly at checkout. Only applicable taxes are added to your final bill.

Can I schedule deliveries in advance?

Yes! You can schedule delivery for a specific date/time during checkout. Choose from available delivery slots in your area.

Do you deliver on holidays?

We deliver on most days. Holiday delivery availability is shown at checkout. For holidays, we may charge a small premium for next-day delivery.
